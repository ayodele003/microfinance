Polaris Global Account

A multi-currency account tailored for Nigerians in the Diaspora. It offers a secure, reliable, and convenient way for customers to manage investments and consummate financial transactions in Nigeria while residing abroad.

Features and Benefits
  • Minimum opening and operating balance of N50,000.
  • Zero Account Maintenance fee if a minimum balance of N50,000 is maintained in the account.
  • Availability of Cheque book.
  • Free access to 24 hour Internet and Mobile Banking services.
  • Opportunity to invest in government bonds.
  • Access to a Dedicated 24-hour Service Desk.
  • Account can also be opened in USD, GBP and EUR.
  • Terms and conditions apply.
Documentation
-Passport photograph & Signature.
-Form of identification as applicable (Nigerian International Passport or Foreign international passport with place of birth as Nigeria).
-Form of address verification (Foreign utility bill).
